###### While researching the case study on UPI/Online Payment Fraud, I was most surprised by how sophisticated "social engineering" has become. I learned that many scammers don't hack the system; they "hack" the person by creating a false sense of urgency or fear.

###### 

###### As a result of this research, one habit I will personally change is my approach to scanning QR codes and sharing OTPs. I will now treat my UPI PIN with the same level of secrecy as a bank password and never enter it to receive money, only to send it. Additionally, I have saved the 1930 Helpline in my contacts to ensure I can act quickly if I or a peer ever encounters a financial threat.

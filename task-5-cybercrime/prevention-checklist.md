# 8 Tips to Stay Safe Online

To protect your digital identity and finances, follow these essential safety protocols:

1. **Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA):** Use an extra layer of security, such as an OTP or an authenticator app, for all sensitive accounts.
2. **Check the Link:** Never click on unsolicited links in SMS or emails. Always verify the sender's email address or the website's URL.
3. **Update Software:** Regularly update your OS and mobile apps to ensure you have the latest security patches against hackers.
4. **Strong Passwords:** Use a unique, complex password for every account. Avoid using easily guessable info like birthdays or names.
5. **Beware of Phishing:** Be skeptical of messages creating a sense of "urgency" or "fear," such as claims that your bank account is being blocked.
6. **UPI Safety - PIN is for Sending Only:** Never enter your UPI PIN to **receive** money. You only need a PIN when you are paying someone else.
7. **UPI Safety - Verify the Recipient:** Always double-check the verified name that appears on the UPI app before clicking the "Pay" button.
8. **Call the 1930 Helpline:** If you are a victim of financial fraud, immediately call the National Cybercrime Helpline at **1930** or report it at **cybercrime.gov.in**.

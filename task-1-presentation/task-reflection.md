######For my assignment as a Student Digital Ambassador I wanted to make something that people would actually want to look at. I did not want it to look like a classroom poster. I used Canva to make a one-page infographic about the three things: Safe Internet Practices, Email Etiquette, and Professional Online Presence. I picked an clean layout because I know my batchmates are more likely to read something with quick icons and bullet points than a big block of text.

######I found it really interesting to think about what Digital Literacy means for a B.Tech student like my batchmates and me today. Digital Literacy is not just about knowing how to use a computer it is about knowing which tools to trust when it comes to literacy.

######The part that was tricky to figure out was the Safe Internet Practices section for my infographic about literacy. I had to remove a lot of words to make sure the advice, like using 2FA and checking URL security for safe browsing was something that a B.Tech student like my batchmates and me could actually do when they are in a hurry, between classes and still practice safe browsing.
